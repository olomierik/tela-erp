## 2. Unique Features for Africa & Asia

### A. Mobile Money as a First-Class Citizen
Unlike Western ERPs that treat mobile money as a "custom plugin," Tela ERP has native support for:
- **STK Push Integration**: Real-time payment prompts on customer phones.
- **Automated Reconciliation**: Matches M-Pesa/Wave/GCash statements with invoices automatically.
- **Offline Recording**: Allows merchants to record mobile money transactions even when the network is down.

### B. The "Market Trader" Quick-POS
A simplified, mobile-first interface designed for high-volume, low-value transactions common in Asian and African markets. 
- Supports Bluetooth thermal printers.
- Works 100% offline.

### C. Currency Resilience Engine
Automatically tracks multiple local currencies and provides "Real-Time Exchange Risk" alerts. This is crucial for businesses in countries with volatile exchange rates (e.g., Nigeria, Lebanon, Sri Lanka).

## 3. Deployment Strategy: The "Offline Node"
Businesses can install a "Tela Node" on a single local laptop. Other devices (tablets, phones) in the shop can connect to this local node via Wi-Fi, even without an external internet connection. This creates a "Local Cloud" that is resilient to national internet outages.
